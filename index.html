About Me

I am a Researcher (Technology R&D Assoc. Principal) at Accenture Technology Labs, Bangalore, India. I am a part of the Conversational AI group, currently leading AI for Sustainability team. I earned my Ph.D. in 2020 from Indian Institute of Technology Patna, India (IITP). My Ph.D. supervisors was Dr. Sriparna Saha.

I broadly work in the area of Multi-objective Optimization, Multi-view Learning, Biomedical, Deep Learning (DL), Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML). My current research focuses on Sustainable AI and Conversational AI
